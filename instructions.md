# Instructions

How to Operate Backtrack

Backtrack is an AI-Notetaker for meetings business men and women have at in-person trade shows and hosted buyer programs. Backtrack is a pre-provisioned app distributed onsite at a trade show; meaning you will be handed a device with the app already installed. Before a meeting starts, Backtrack’s AI will build context on the person you’re meeting with via their business card. During the meeting the app will take notes for you. After the meeting ends, users are emailed a summary, transcription, and notes of their meeting. 


Step 1. Click on the blue and white icon that looks like a cursive B.


Figure 1. This is the Backtrack app icon. (Source: Jordan Walker).

Step 2. Tap the Sign In button.

Step 3. Click in the username box and type your pre-provisioned username.

Step 4. Click in the password box and type your pre-provisioned password.

Step 5. Tap New Meeting button at the bottom of the screen.

Step 6. Look for the app to open a screen containing your phone’s camera. 

Step 7. Put your client’s business card in the camera frame. 


Figure 2. This is the camera frame with a business card in it. (Source: Jordan Walker).

Step 8. Tap the Scan and Start Recording button to begin recording your meeting.

Step 9. Leave the phone running for the duration of the meeting.


Figure 3. The app is running. (Source: Jordan Walker).

Step 10. Tap End Meeting when the meeting is over.

Step 11. The app will reset itself for your next meeting.

Step 12. AI notes from your meeting will automatically be emailed to you.
